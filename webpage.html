About THE BODY SHOP


The Body Shop International Limited, commonly known as The Body Shop, is a British firm that specializes in skin care, makeup, and fragrances.
Established by Anita Roddick in 1976, the firm presently offers 1,000 products for sale in around 3,000 stores, which are split between company-owned and franchised locations across over 65 countries.

The founder, Anita Roddick, started it all when she opened a small, green store in Brighton with the innovative notion that commerce should be a force for good.
With over 40 years of lobbying, creating change, and shattering standards in the beauty industry, we've never been your typical cosmetics firm, and we're still going strong.
There has always been more to The Body Shop than just cosmetics. It served a purpose by balancing beliefs with profit and they never used animal testing or forced labor to create any of our products.

The Body Shop works to create a more equitable and attractive world. This serves as our mission and motivates all we accomplish. Our three core beliefs—that business can be a force for good, that girls and women should be empowered, and that everyone is beautiful—are everything to us.
